# Venture OS Implementation Roadmap

## Phase 0: Product Definition

Status: in progress.

Deliverables:

- Product spec.
- Ecosystem client map.
- Module/workflow spec.
- Agentic team model.
- Data model.
- Pilot onboarding plan.
- GTM packaging.

Acceptance:

- GTCX repos mapped as internal pilot clients.
- Fundraising engine linked as internal source module.
- Warm client onboarding flow defined.

## Phase 1: Internal Pilot Across GTCX Portfolio

Objective: run Venture OS for 3-5 GTCX product repos as first clients.

Recommended pilots:

1. `terra-os`: sovereign/DFI/bank pilot path.
2. `markets-os`: buyer/revenue/dealer-broker path.
3. `compliance-os`: enterprise trust/compliance path.
4. `terminal-os` + `griot-ai`: visibility/content path.
5. `nyota-ai`: program/channel partnership path.

Deliverables:

- Client profiles.
- Product positioning sheets.
- Opportunity pipelines.
- First collateral packs.
- Weekly founder briefs.
- Warm outreach lists.

Acceptance:

- 5 client profiles complete.
- 50 opportunities routed across clients.
- 10 outreach/collateral assets drafted.
- 5 approved external moves.
- 0 external actions without approval.

## Phase 2: Warm Client Managed Service

Objective: onboard external warm clients as a high-touch managed service.

Deliverables:

- Client intake form.
- ClickUp template.
- Gmail/Calendar/Drive setup guide.
- Weekly client brief template.
- Monthly investor update template.
- Pricing/options memo.

Acceptance:

- 2-3 warm clients onboarded.
- Each client receives weekly operating brief.
- Each client has active capital, revenue, partnership, and visibility pipeline.
- At least one measurable outcome per client within 30 days.

## Phase 3: Automation Layer

Objective: reduce manual operating burden.

Deliverables:

- n8n source listener workflows.
- Gmail to CRM workflow.
- Calendar to meeting-prep workflow.
- Daily digest automation.
- Search sweep workflow.
- Dedupe rules.

Acceptance:

- Inbound email creates or updates client/opportunity tasks.
- Source listeners produce deduped candidates.
- Daily digest runs on schedule.
- Automation never bypasses approval gates.

## Phase 4: Venture OS App MVP

Objective: build an internal app that becomes the product control plane.

MVP Modules:

1. Clients.
2. Sources.
3. Signals.
4. Opportunities.
5. Organizations.
6. Relationships.
7. Deliverables.
8. Approvals.
9. Digest.
10. Analytics.

Acceptance:

- App stores client and opportunity data.
- ClickUp sync creates/updates execution tasks.
- Client command center shows priorities, deadlines, approvals, and blockers.
- Weekly client report can be generated from app data.

## Phase 5: Cohort / Studio Product

Objective: support accelerators, venture studios, funds, and hubs.

Deliverables:

- Cohort dashboard.
- Portfolio company profiles.
- Shared opportunity database.
- Intro management.
- Cohort visibility calendar.
- Founder progress reports.

Acceptance:

- One cohort or studio can run 10+ client profiles.
- Operators can compare pipeline, readiness, and outcomes.
- Founders receive individualized briefs.

## Phase 6: Intelligence Network

Objective: make Venture OS a defensible intelligence layer.

Deliverables:

- Africa/Global South source graph.
- Country, sector, and capital-source maps.
- Investor and partner relationship graph.
- Event/program calendar.
- Visibility and press opportunity graph.
- Outcome analytics.

Acceptance:

- 1,000+ monitored sources.
- 500+ organizations.
- 100+ recurring queries.
- 54-country coverage roadmap operational.
- Outcome data improves scoring and recommendations.
