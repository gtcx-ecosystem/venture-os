# Venture OS Roadmap

Venture OS is the GTCX venture and partnership acceleration lane: ecosystem GTM, alliance building, and institutional pilot commercialization.

This `agile/roadmaps/product/` hub keeps product roadmap coordination outside `docs/product/`. Venture work is tracked in business strategy, GTM, and cross-repo coordination handoffs, then handed to scrum through feature packs.

## Canonical roadmap artifacts

- **Business strategy:** [`docs/business/strategy/`](../../../docs/business/strategy/)
- **GTM register:** [`docs/gtm/`](../../../docs/gtm/)
- **Coordination handoffs:** [`docs/operations/coordination/`](../../../docs/operations/coordination/)

## Lane policy

`venture-os` does not maintain an engineering story backlog under `docs/product/`. GTM, legal, enterprise procurement, security, and compliance tracks are separate operational lanes owned by venture-os operations and surfaced as coordination handoffs to implementing repos.
