# Pricing and options memo — Venture OS managed service

**Effective:** 2026-06-17  
**Audience:** Warm client founders evaluating managed venture-growth operations

## Service tiers

| Tier | Cadence | Includes | Best for |
|------|---------|----------|----------|
| **Pilot** | 5-day onboarding + 4 weeks | Intake, pipelines, rolling brief, approval gates | First warm client proving fit |
| **Operating** | Weekly brief + daily digest | ClickUp/Gmail/Calendar rails, n8n automations, collateral support | Active raise or GTM sprint |
| **Studio** | Dedicated operator + cohort | Multi-desk orchestration, investor updates, program applications | Portfolio companies in motion |

## Pricing bands (categorical)

| Band | Scope | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| **FOSS-first** | Tooling at $0 license cost | n8n self-hosted, open CRM, GitBook |
| **Low-touch SaaS** | Per-seat productivity | Gmail Workspace, ClickUp, Notion |
| **Managed uplift** | Operator time + approval SLA | Priced per client complexity, not per seat |

Numeric quotes are client-specific after Day 0 intake (stage, desk mix, automation depth).

## Options matrix

| Option | Automation | Approval model | Data residency |
|--------|------------|----------------|----------------|
| A — Manual rails | Docs + app only | Founder approves all outbound | Client-owned Google |
| B — Semi-automated | n8n digest + inbound capture | Venture OS gates publish | GTCX-hosted n8n |
| C — Full studio | Source listeners + dedupe | Tiered approvers per desk | Fabric receipt ledger |

## Acceptance criteria (Phase 2)

- 2–3 warm clients onboarded on **Pilot** or **Operating**
- Weekly operating brief delivered per client
- At least one measurable outcome per client within 30 days
