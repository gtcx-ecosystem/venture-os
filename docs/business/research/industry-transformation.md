# Industry Transformation

## Industry shift

AI-native companies are moving from human-operated SaaS stacks toward human-agent operating systems.

The shift is not just automation. It changes the operating model:

1. Agents become governed actors with identity, permissions, tools, and evidence.
2. Company knowledge becomes a graph, not only a wiki or vector store.
3. Workflows require provenance and replay, not only task completion.
4. Human judgment, leadership, ethics, and culture become explicit operating assets.
5. Innovation, creative expression, delivery, services, and assurance converge into one loop.

Ecosystem OS should position itself around this transformation.
