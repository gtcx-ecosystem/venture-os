# Funding Analysis

## Funding landscape

Investor interest is flowing into agent platforms, enterprise graph, workflow automation, governance, and AI-native business operations.

Ecosystem OS should frame funding around a category thesis:

```text
AI-native companies need operating systems, not isolated copilots.
```

Relevant funding narratives:

1. Enterprise AI graph and search platforms show demand for company intelligence.
2. Agentic workflow companies show demand for governed automation.
3. Service operations and AI control tower platforms show enterprise appetite for operational control.
4. HR and finance systems show the value of becoming a system of record.
5. Open-source agent frameworks show developer appetite, but lack full company operating context.

The wedge is an internally proven operating system with open-source primitives and enterprise-grade control surfaces.
