# Master Voice

## One-line

Ecosystem OS is the operating system for AI-native companies.

## Short paragraph

Ecosystem OS connects company intelligence, human judgment, agent coordination, delivery, services, assurance, innovation, and market expression into one governed operating platform. It is built on Kernel, Graph, Ledger, and Surface, with operating modules for Ethos, Venture, Agency, Bridge, Agile, Fabric, and Assurance.

## Boundary language

Canon publishes. Ecosystem operates. GTCX builds trade infrastructure. Baseline powers AI runtime.

## Avoid

1. Do not call Ecosystem OS a documentation hub.
2. Do not describe Graph as RAG.
3. Do not reduce Assurance to compliance only.
4. Do not reduce Ethos to HR.
5. Do not collapse Venture and Agency.
